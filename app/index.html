Before we begin

A quick read so you know what Tether is — and what it isn’t.

Tether is coaching, not therapy.

Tether is an educational coaching tool for building resilience, communication skills, and self-leadership through workplace change. It is not therapy, counseling, medical care, crisis support, or a substitute for a licensed mental health professional. If you’re in crisis or thinking about harming yourself or someone else, please contact your EAP, HR, or call or text 988 (U.S. Suicide & Crisis Lifeline).

Your conversations are private from your employer.

Your chat messages, check-in responses, and session summaries are stored on Supabase (US-East) so you can return to them. Your employer only ever sees anonymized, aggregated insights — never your individual conversations, name, or email — and only when at least 5 employees have contributed data. You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing the address provided by your company.

Some topics are out of scope.

If you bring up self-harm, suicidal thoughts, abuse, harm to others, diagnosable conditions, or active crisis, Tether will acknowledge what you’re sharing and redirect you to a professional resource. Tether does not diagnose, prescribe, treat, or intervene in a crisis.

How your data is used.

Your data is used only to (a) deliver your coaching experience, (b) generate the anonymized, aggregate insights your company’s HR or L&D team sees, and (c) improve the product. We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it to train third-party AI models.

By continuing, you confirm you’re using Tether voluntarily and you’re at least 18 years old.
