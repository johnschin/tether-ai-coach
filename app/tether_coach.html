Why Tether

Not an EAP. Not a meditation app. Not surveillance.

EAPs are reactive — someone has to be struggling before they get referred. Meditation apps are generic — they can't help you navigate a conversation with your new manager after a reorg. And most enterprise AI deployments feel like surveillance, not support. Tether is different.

What exists today

EAPs wait for crisis. Wellness apps offer generic breathing exercises. Enterprise tools ban or restrict AI — signaling control, not trust. Employees are left to navigate reorgs, role changes, and uncertainty on their own, with no practical support for the specific situation they're actually in.

What Tether does

Meets employees where they are — in the actual situation they're facing — with practical, scenario-specific coaching grounded in change management science and evidence-based psychology. Proactive, private, available 24/7. Your company gets anonymous aggregate insights. Your employees get a coach that actually helps.

It works for managers too

Tether helps leaders lead change better.

Tether isn't just for individual contributors navigating uncertainty. Managers use it to pressure-test their change communication, identify blind spots, and find better framing before rolling changes out to their teams.
